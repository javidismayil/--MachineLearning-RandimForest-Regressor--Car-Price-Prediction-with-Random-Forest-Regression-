Fuel type: A column specifying the type of fuel used in the vehicle. Examples include Gasoline, Diesel, Electric, etc.

Engine volume: A column indicating the engine displacement, typically expressed in liters.

Mileage: A column indicating the mileage of the vehicle, often measured in kilometers.

Cylinders: A column specifying the number of cylinders in the vehicle's engine, usually taking values like 4, 6, or 8.

Gearbox type: A column indicating the type of gearbox in the vehicle, such as Manual, Automatic, Semi-Automatic, etc.

Drive wheels: A column specifying the drive or traction wheels of the vehicle, such as Front-Wheel Drive (FWD), Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD), All-Wheel Drive (AWD), etc.

Doors: A column indicating the number of doors in the vehicle, often listed as 2-door, 4-door, etc.

Wheel: A column specifying the position of the steering wheel, whether it's Left-Hand Drive (LHD) or Right-Hand Drive (RHD).

Color: A column indicating the color of the vehicle.

Airbags: A column specifying the number of airbags in the vehicle, typically represented as a numeric value.
